## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I create a recipe?
1. Tap the "+" button on the Recipes screen
2. Enter the recipe name and details
3. Add ingredients with quantities
4. Write the preparation steps
5. Save your recipe

### How do I manage my inventory?
Go to the Inventory tab to:
- Add new ingredients with their quantities
- Update stock levels
- Set expiration dates
- Get low stock alerts

### How does the Premium subscription work?
Premium gives you access to:
- Unlimited recipes (free version limited to 10)
- Advanced inventory management
- Cost tracking and analytics
- Recipe sharing features
- Priority support

### How much does Premium cost?
- Monthly: €4.99/month
- Annual: €39.99/year (save 33%)

### How do I cancel my subscription?
1. Open iPhone Settings
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select "Chef Helper"
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
